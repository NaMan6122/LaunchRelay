Built for founders, by founders

LaunchRelay is a free, reciprocal traffic-exchange network designed to solve the cold-start problem for indie founders.

The Core Mechanic

The idea is simple: you display another founder's startup on your site, and yours gets shown on theirs. Every impression is reciprocal by design — the network only works when everyone participates.

But not all traffic exchanges are equal. Most classic traffic exchanges suffered from quality collapse — irrelevant matches, bot clicks, and members who quietly remove their embed after joining. LaunchRelay was built from day one to avoid that fate.

Relevance

Category-aware matching ensures your widget shows startups in related spaces, not random pairings.

Fairness

Traffic-weighted reciprocity means members who send more real traffic earn proportionally more back.

Trust

Automated re-crawling detects removed embeds. Grace periods for honest mistakes, de-listing for churners.

How Reciprocity Works

Rather than a flat "one embed = one placement," each startup accrues a reciprocity balance:

balance = impressions_given (verified) − impressions_received
  • Positive balance (giving more than receiving) → prioritized for placement on other sites. You're "owed" visibility.
  • Negative balance → deprioritized until you catch back up.

This naturally rewards high-traffic members without requiring any payment or bidding system.

Matching Score

When the engine picks a partner to show on your site, it scores candidates using:

score = 0.4 × category_overlap
       + 0.3 × normalize(balance)
       + 0.2 × trust_score
       + 0.1 × diversity_penalty

Weights are tunable and will be adjusted based on early network CTR data.

Trust & Safety

The network is protected by multiple layers of automated defense:

Embed Verification

A background job periodically fetches each member's site and confirms the widget script is still present. Absence triggers a grace period notification, then automatic de-listing.

Viewport-Only Impressions

Impressions only count after 1 second of continuous visibility inside the viewport (via IntersectionObserver). Hidden or off-screen widgets don't generate credit.

Rate Limiting

Per-endpoint rate limits prevent abuse: 10 req/min for match requests, 60 req/min for impressions, 30 req/min for clicks.

Trust Score

Every startup has a 0–1 trust score computed from domain age, re-crawl pass rate, and review flags. Low-trust members are deprioritized in matching.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LaunchRelay really free?
Yes, the core reciprocal exchange is free forever. We may offer optional paid features in the future (priority placement, deeper analytics), but the core mechanic will always be free.
Will the widget slow down my site?
No. The widget is under 3KB gzipped, loads asynchronously, uses Shadow DOM for style isolation, and reserves a fixed-height slot to prevent layout shift. Your Core Web Vitals stay green.
What happens if I remove the widget?
Our automated re-crawl will detect the removal. You'll get a grace period to fix it (in case it was accidental). If the widget isn't restored, your listing gets de-listed from the network.
Can I choose which startups appear on my site?
Not individually, but you can exclude specific categories or domains you don't want to be matched with. The matching engine handles the rest, balancing relevance, reciprocity, and diversity.
How is this different from an ad network?
Ad networks sell impressions to the highest bidder. LaunchRelay is reciprocal — you only appear on other founders' sites, and only if you're also showing someone else. The audience is self-selecting and relevant.
What platforms does the widget work on?
Anywhere you can paste a script tag — static HTML, Next.js, Webflow, WordPress, Shopify, Ghost, and more. No framework dependencies.
